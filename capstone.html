-HIS LITTLE SWEETHEART-

The person he longs to meet always,
Perhaps the sole one he wishes to gaze;
Even for a moment can't drift apart,
She is none other than his Little Sweetheart.

An Epitome amongst the Fickle girls,
She's the one who neither Rages nor Hurls;
Anger though is Tipped on her Nose,
But Consoling her had become his habitual Course.

Just a small Glance of her Colossal Smile,
Relieves all the Stresses for a little while;
Her madness, witty Jokes and all Crazy stuff,
Is all he needs at night, for his Tired mind to Bluff.

A Chest, full of Talks & Memories she is,
With loads of hundred Ever-Lasting Topics;
But now with each day adding to the Almanac,
New Promisinng Memories in future to throwback.

Dangerous she is, if with her you Mess,
Just a 'Sam-stare' will get you in distress;
Truly speaking, She's a lot Polite and Humble,
For she accepts any damn thing without a Grumble.

Making things look simple and with Ease she does,
Without losing her Calm but with a constant buzz;
Always willing to Lend a hand to a needy; while;
She just loves to be their Reason of smile.

Filled with Naughtiness & Adventurous in nature,
Always on her Toes to get on a Venture;
She's his little girl with Heck lots of Dislikes,
With his opnions always Countering her Likes.

Careless she is as far as Health is concerned,
But hugely Affectionate and Caring towards her beloved person;
This is all about his Sweetheart, who's a Gem of a girl,
And he'll treat her just as an Oyster treats a Pearl..!
