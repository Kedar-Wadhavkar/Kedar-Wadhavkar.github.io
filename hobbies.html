-HIS JOURNEY TOWARDS HIS LOVE-

She didn't believe in love,
It's charming & beautiful as a dove;
He wanted a relationship very late,
She thought being single was in her fate.

It was a day when he made her realize,
That there isn't a better feeling than love to apprize;
The next day when he confessed, he loved her,
She was on the ninth cloud, even more happier.

Days kept rolling one after the other,
Building their relation more firm & stronger;
With many sweet moments coming their way,
Making each day more exotic and gay.

Until an evening after a long hectic day,
She exclaimed that something she wanted to say;
She's in love with him, she expressed blushing,
The truth beneath her eyes was just overwhelming.

It was the moment most awaited for,
He just couldn't believe what he'd heard so far;
Yeah, said she with a huge blushed smile,
Which drove him to heaven for a little while.

Since then his life changed its priority,
With all his dreams transforming into reality;
Morning till night with each other,
They ceaselessly chattered whatsoever.

One day she'll propose him for sure though,
Perhaps keeps him waiting, as a sign of true love;
But they'd certainly be in a relation-ship soon,
A Ship on a voyage of life, with a honey and a moon..!

-HIS LITTLE SWEETHEART-

The person he longs to meet always,
Perhaps the sole one he wishes to gaze;
Even for a moment can't drift apart,
She is none other than his little sweetheart.

An epitome amongst the fickle girls,
She's the one who neither rages nor hurls;
Anger though is tipped on her nose,
But consoling her had become his habitual course.

Just a small glance of her colossal smile,
Relieves all the stresses for a little while;
Her madness, witty jokes and all crazy stuff,
Is all he needs at night, for his tired mind to bluff.

A chest, full of talks & memories she is,
With loads of hundred ever-lasting topics;
But now with each day adding to the almanac,
New promisinng memories in future to throwback.

Making things look simple and with ease she does,
Without losing her calm but with a constant buzz;
Always willing to lend a hand to a needy; while;
She just loves to be their reason of smile.

Filled with naughtiness & adventurous in nature,
Always on her toes to get on a venture;
She's his little girl with heck lots of dislikes,
With his opnions always countering her likes.

Careless she is as far as health is concerned,
But hugely affectionate and caring towards her beloved person;
This is all about his sweetheart, who's a gem of a girl,
And he'll treat her just as an oyster treats a pearl..!

-HAPPY BIRTHDAY PRINCESS-

Today's the day when the 'beautiful you' were born,
A blessing from the Almighty, to your family to adorn;
Wishing you a very very happy birthday,
With lots of love & wishes specially today.

It's solely your day, enjoy to the fullest dear,
Marking the beginning of an another magnificent year;
Loads of desires and wishes to fulfill,
Creating and enhancing memories at your will.

May God shower all his blessings upon you,
May you conquer all the success, you aimed to;
May everything come true that you dreamt in your head,
May you lead a very successful & prosperous year ahead.

Have an awesome journey ahead, full of joy & wealth,
But most importantly, stay in the pink of your health;
Wish you again, many many happy returns of the day,
From the bottom of my heart, with gratitude I say..!
